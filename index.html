Professional development, solved.

We are a workforce optimization company that helps employees master their current role and prepare for future challenges.

Stay Updated

PERSONALIZED SKILLS FEED

Employees and their managers pick skills which will be prioritized and presented to the employee in the form of a feed. From there, they can study the skill, take checkpoint quizzes, take a test, and complete real-life exercises to reinforce their knowledge of the topic.

ROLE-BASED DEVELOPMENT

Our goal is to make employees the best they can be in their current role. Beyond skill development, we help people identify and destroy roadblocks, request feedback from managers and peers, track 1:1 feedback, and manage links to important documents that help them do their jobs.

BEYOND SOFTWARE

We’re not just another software company. We believe software is an enabler of professional development, but people need much more than what can be learned on the computer screen. That’s why we develop offline team-based activities to reinforce knowledge learned with CareerSofia.
